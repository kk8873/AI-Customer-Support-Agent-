# Refund Policy — KaranKart Electronics

This policy governs refunds for orders placed through KaranKart. All amounts are in
Indian Rupees (INR).

## 1. Return window
Refunds are available within **30 days of the delivery date**, extended to **45 days
for VIP customers**. Requests made after the applicable window has closed are not
eligible.

## 2. Sealed items
A factory-sealed, unopened item is eligible for a **full refund** within the return
window.

## 3. Opened items
An opened item is eligible **only if it is defective or dead-on-arrival (DOA)**.
Opened items in working condition are not refundable.

## 4. Final-sale items
Items marked **final sale, clearance, or open-box are non-refundable**, with no
exceptions — including defective claims.

## 5. High-value orders
Refunds **above ₹50,000 require manager approval**. These are escalated to a manager
rather than approved or denied automatically.

## 6. Ownership
A refund is processed only for an order that **exists** and **belongs to the
requesting customer**.

## Outcomes
- **Approved** — meets all conditions and is within the auto-approval limit.
- **Denied** — violates one or more of the conditions above, or required information is missing or cannot be verified.
- **Escalated** — otherwise valid, but exceeds the ₹50,000 approval limit.
